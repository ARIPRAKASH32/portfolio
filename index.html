A place for learning, innovation and expression:

We’re focused on engineering that matters — on providing students with the skills and knowledge needed to bring in the change that’s required, and the courage to do it. At Shanmugha College of Engineering and Technology, we value collaboration, community-engaged learning and adaptation to change and strive to develop engineers who are socially and globally aware, understand business and entrepreneurship, are prepared to take on roles and are conscious of the consequences of their work — to society, the environment and the profession. Engineering has many disciplines and we provide a varied number of programs. Each of our programs combines stellar academic education with practical application, thus ensuring you graduate ready to thrive in this evolving field.
